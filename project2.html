Senior University Website

Challenge Statement

Create a user-friendly website for a senior university to cater to retirees, ensuring simplicity and accessibility.

Solution Statement

The website design is simplified with larger fonts to accommodate seniors with vision difficulties. It includes video guides and an option to read aloud the page content. These features ensure the site is accessible and easy to navigate for older users. I used clear, large typography and a straightforward layout to enhance readability and usability.
