Pet Adoption App

Challenge Statement

Design a modern and user-friendly mobile app interface to enhance the experience of adopting pets, particularly appealing to first-time users.

Solution Statement

I used Figma to design a cute and animal-themed interface to attract users. The design includes playful icons and a soft color palette to reduce visual fatigue. By analyzing user behavior data, I discovered that most users prefer one-handed operation, so I optimized the button sizes and positions to ensure easy use.
